Our Mission

About Hridaya

Hridaya (Hindi for "heart") is a research-driven emotional wellness assistant built to solve a fundamental problem: we forget how we feel.

After a difficult week, patients often struggle to recall small but significant emotional moments during therapy. Hridaya captures them every day ‚Äî through natural conversation ‚Äî so nothing important is lost.

Our multi-modal AI fuses text sentiment (RoBERTa), LLM-scored emotional context, and voice tone analysis (Librosa + Random Forest) for a holistic picture that no single model can achieve alone.
